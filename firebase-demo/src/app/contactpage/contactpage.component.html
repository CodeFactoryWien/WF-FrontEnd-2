About Us



Founded in 2016, Pro Gaming Academy Wien follows the concept of pro gaming boot camps of Anglo-American countries. Our goal is to provide time-efficient and very practice-oriented training with the aim of the fastest shot. Our courses are tailored to noobs, people who want to change careers in the second or third educational path as well as people who are interested in an Pro Gamer education.
